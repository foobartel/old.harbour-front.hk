Talk: 8:00pm

30 Products 30 Days: Learn UX by doing UX.

How do you learn UX? As a teacher of UX, I'm often tasked with helping to impart a designer-ly view of the world into the mind of students. Although each student learns differently, one common factor applies to all students: learning by applying.

In this 40 minute talk, User Experience practitioner and teacher, Kristin Low, will share methods for how anyone can learn and apply the principles of UX in as rapid an amount of time as possible.
