Harbour Front Monthly #1

Wednesday, AUGUST 27, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Welcome Harbour Front Monthly!

Harbour Front Monthly is a new event series that will cover a variety of topics around all things mobile & responsive web design & development, discussing the latest technologies, new ideas and best practices in web design. The event format will consist of 1 or 2 presentations of approx. 30 minutes each, followed by a short Q&A and networking drinks to mingle with your industry peers afterwards.

The first event will take place on Wednesday, AUGUST 27, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM at the Hive in Wan Chai. (HK$100 includes free drinks)

You can register via Eventbrite or at the door on the evening of the event. You can also add yourself on Lanyrd and follow us on Twitter for the latest updates.

Talk 1: 7:00pm

Don't Overwrite My Stuff!

An Introduction to Git for Front End Web Design by Bart Verkoeijen

Bart is a software developer building web, apps, and cloud. Covering application logic, interface, architecture, and IT infrastructure from front end to back end, ensuring that end-users and customers achieve their goals through software. In the process he wears several hats; Developer, System Architect, DevOp, and IT Administrator.

In recent years he has built a hybrid mobile app, Surround App, allowing foreigners to engage on Chinese social media. A Chinese slang database, SlangPros, and a cloud back end to support the two. He recently joined Quantifeed to work on their wealth investment web app.

Currently, Bart is working in his spare time on a new startup; Flippercast, a new JavaScript based open source content management system, incorporating best practices he learned over the years.

@bgever

Talk 2: 7:40pm

Building Better Responsive Sites

Tips, Tricks & Advice to Improve Your Responsive Design Projects by Holger Bartel

Holger is a web design consultant and advises on strategy as well as design, and develops responsive and mobile websites with a focus on performance.

He also founded and runs the Open Device Lab HK, works as a part-time lecturer at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University's school of design and mentors for startups as well as speaks at international conferences on the topics of web design.

@foobartel
