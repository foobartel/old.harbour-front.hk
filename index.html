The StartmeupHK Week Edition

We're doubling the fun - two Events!

This month, we're bringing you two days of our monthly event, this time as part of StartmeupHK Week. As usual, we will be covering a variety of topics around all things web design & development. What can you expect? A nice crowd. Beers, soda's and munchies. Sit back and relax while you learn new things and meet new people at the lovely co-working space The Hive.

The first event will take place on Tuesday, November 11, 2014 from 7:00pm to 9:00pm at the Hive in Wan Chai.

Our second event will take place on Thursday, November 13, 2014 from 7:00pm to 9:00pm and is also hosted at the Hive in Wan Chai.

You can register via Eventbrite or at the door on the evening of the event. You can follow us on Twitter for the latest updates and we're on Meetup too.
